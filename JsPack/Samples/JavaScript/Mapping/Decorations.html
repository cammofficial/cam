About this sample

This sample shows the different types of decorations, supported by JsMapping.
Decoration layers can display Marker and Bubble objects, associated with a given geographic location. Markers can show a custom icon, specified by the imageSrc property, or they can be styled by css. The string specified by the text property will be shown as a hint when the marker is hovered. Bubbles display the plain text or HTML string, specified by their text property.
The built-in layers list controller shows the list of all layers (map layers on top and overlays below) and provides an easy way to control their visibility.
